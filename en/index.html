Jordan Schneider

I’m Jordan Schneider, an aspiring electrical engineer with a background in programming.

I am currently a fourth-year student at the University of Melbourne undertaking a Bachelor of Science. In my spare time, I like to work on various personal projects, like an Ardiuno robot and creating a watchface for the Pebble smartwatch. I have an interest in languages and linguistics, and am fluent in four languages: English, German, Chinese and Japanese.

I was born and grew up in Australia, but my travels have taken me around the globe to Taiwan, Mainland China and Japan. These experiences abroad have allowed me to develop an international outlook and gain insight into different customs and cultures.
